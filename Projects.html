Broken Souls Rescue

This project was part of my social media course at the university. Our task was to reach out to an organization and provide social media support throughout a set time period. I had chosen to work with an animal shelter and assist in the promotion of a giftshop. Apart from the gift shop, I had created promotional footage for the charity to release as well as a series on all social media platforms showcasing specific rescued animals the organization had fostered and given a better life.
